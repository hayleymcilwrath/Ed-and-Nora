About us

The aim of Ed and Nora Sensory Adventures is to help support parents in preparing their children for various social outings. Any activity for a child with autism or a fear, that is not part of their everyday routine, can prove to be extremely stressful for all involved. For example, going to the hairdressers. Every child will need a haircut at some point in their life and going to the hairdressers can be an extremely stressful sensory experience for children with ASD.

This can be due to the fact that in most cases it is a sensory overload. New smells, touching to the head, sounds, people, noises and uncertainty of exactly what the scissors or shaver will do. This causes unnecessary stress on the children. We provide a tool to help prepare the child beforehand by familiarising them with the sensory experiences involved. We want them to experience the smells, the sounds, be able to touch and smell the real tools being used on each adventure.

Here at Ed and Nora we offer a support fund to help support parents and children with ASD. All monies raised will be available in small grants which parents can apply for. We care deeply about supporting the parents and want to help by donating a percentage of all sales towards this fund.
